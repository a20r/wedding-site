Our Story

Rooted in the Green Mountains

From trail days that turned into late-night bonfires, to countless slices of American Flatbread, Vermont has been the backdrop to our favorite memories. We can’t wait to share the magic of the Mad River Valley with the people we love most.

Why Vermont

We fell in love with this valley long before we fell for each other. It’s where the maples glow gold, the rivers run cold, and every dirt road seems to lead to something a little wild and wonderful.

The Proposal

On a misty morning hike above Waitsfield, we found a quiet overlook, unwrapped thermoses of coffee, and decided that adventures are better when we plan them together for good.

What to Expect

Think farm-to-table feasts, Vermont craft brews, crackling campfires, and music that keeps you dancing. Dress to feel great outdoors—and pack layers for those crisp mountain nights.
