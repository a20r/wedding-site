Our Story

From chance encounters on a frosty Vermont lift line to late-night campfire plans, our story has always been rooted in the mountains. We fell in love exploring winding singletrack, trading homemade maple lattes, and sharing the quiet rhythm of small town life. On July 11, 2026 we get to bring everyone we love to the heart of it all: Lareau Farm in Waitsfield.

We're so grateful that you're making the trip to celebrate with us among green hills, big skies, and the scent of spruce after a summer rain.

Travel & Stay

Waitsfield sits in the Mad River Valley, just 45 minutes from Burlington International Airport (BTV). Rental cars are available at the airport, and ride shares operate sporadically—we recommend booking transportation in advance.

For lodging, explore the charming inns and lodges nearby. We've reserved room blocks at:

  • Pitcher Inn — elegant rooms in Warren village
  • Lareau Farm Inn — stay right on the property
  • 1824 House Inn — riverside rooms with breakfast

There are also plenty of cozy Airbnbs tucked along the valley roads. Summer is a popular season, so book early.

MTB in the Mad River Valley

We couldn't resist a little singletrack love note. If you're packing your bike, here's where to roll.

  • Blueberry Lake Trails — flowy loops perfect for a warm-up lap.
  • Eurich Pond Network — classic Vermont tech with mossy rock gardens.
  • Sunny Z & Evolution at Sugarbush — lift-served thrills with Green Mountain views.

Trail Tips

Trails are maintained by the Mad River Riders. Consider a day pass or donation if you ride. Expect slick roots if it rained the night before and keep an eye out for maple creemees as your post-ride reward.
